Hello! I am Inés Galletly, a geographer and Geographic Information Systems (GIS) specialist with a deep passion for cartography. My work focuses on exploring the infinite possibilities that maps offer in the social and environmental fields, fusing science and creativity to create visual representations that tell stories. I specialize in transforming complex data into clear and attractive infographics, facilitating informed decision making and fostering a greater understanding of the environment around us.
